What's Happening?

Pheme allows you to visualize the world around you using live streaming data from Twitter.

Stream Tweets Live » Watch Pre-Recorded Events »

  • Watch Clusters Form

    Points will fall as data from Twitter arrives. As Pheme detects events, it will highlight them in a different color.

  • Explore The Tweets

    Hover over tweets to explore the data in depth.

  • Discover Events

    The sidebar allows you to drill down on an individual cluster to figure out what's happening at the event.
